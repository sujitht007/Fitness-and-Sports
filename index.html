Body Building

Bodybuilding involves strength training to build muscle mass and improve overall physique. It requires a disciplined regimen of exercise, nutrition, and recovery.

Fitness

Fitness is the state of being physically healthy and strong, achieved through regular exercise, balanced nutrition, and maintaining overall well-being. It encompasses muscular strength, flexibility, and bodycomposition.

Boxing

Boxing is a combat sport where two opponents face off in a ring, using punches and defensive techniques to score points or achieve a knockout. It requires a mix of strength, speed, endurance, and strategy.

Crossfit

CrossFit is a high-intensity fitness program that combines elements of weightlifting, cardio, and functional movements. It's designed to improve overall fitness, including strength, endurance, flexibility, and agility, through constantly varied workouts.
